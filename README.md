# Fitness Planner

A comprehensive web application for fitness planning, tracking, and management.

## Features

### Fitness Assessment
- Complete a detailed fitness assessment form to calculate your BMR, TDEE, and target calories
- Input personal information, activity levels, and fitness goals
- Supports both metric and imperial units

### Results Dashboard
- View your calculated daily, weekly, and target calories
- Track your progress with personalized metrics
- Visual representation of macronutrient distribution

### Workout Plans
- Personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals and preferences
- Supports both gym and home workout options
- Structured push/pull/legs split with Week A and B variations
- Appropriate exercises based on experience level

### Meal Plans
- Customized meal plans based on your calorie goals and dietary preferences
- Filter by dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, etc.)
- Detailed recipes with ingredients and instructions
- Calorie and macronutrient information for each meal

## Mobile Responsiveness
The application is fully responsive and works on all devices:
- Mobile-friendly navigation with slide-down menu
- Appropriately sized content for smaller screens
- Touch-friendly buttons and interactive elements

## Getting Started
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Complete the fitness assessment form
3. View your results and access your personalized workout and meal plans

## Technologies Used
- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript (ES6+)
- Local Storage for data persistence
